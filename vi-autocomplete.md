#vi-autocomplete



##commands

####AUTOCOMPLETE

**ctrl-n** list of words in path/file

**ctrl-x-ctrl-f** for filenames in path

**ctrl-p** word completion

**ctrl-p** search back through cmd's (same as arrow up)

**ctrl-n** search forward (same as arrow up)
